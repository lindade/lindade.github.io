About

My focus lies in UX-Research, digital product design and web front-end development. In previous projects I worked on concepts and implementation for mobile applications and websites. Additionally I am curious about AR and VR technologies. I enjoy to work in multinational surroundings. Therefore I've obtained a great international background by living in different countries around the world. I attended universities in South Korea and Germany and gained valuable working experience in Spain and Germany.

Best Neighbors

Best Neighbors

User Research based software-application concept to help neigborhoods to improve living together in their area. The application helps customers to find small stores which dont have any internet representation of their own.
